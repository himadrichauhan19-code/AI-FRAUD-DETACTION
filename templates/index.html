AI Fraud Detection in E-Payments

When payments move fast, fraud moves faster.

This website is built to protect digital payment systems from fraudulent activity in real time. FraudShield studies transaction behavior before settlement and identifies suspicious patterns like unusual amount spikes, odd timing, location drift, risky transfer channels, and unknown merchant behavior.

The goal is simple: reduce financial fraud without blocking genuine users. Investigators can validate flagged transactions, and the model keeps improving from that feedback cycle.
